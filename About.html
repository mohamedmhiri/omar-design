Conduct



Article 1: Conduct

The main idea of ​​the application should be around these areas:
-Tourism
-Health
-Services Banking and Finance
-Education

Each institute will have to be presented by a team that does not exceed 6 people, their work should be distributed as follows:

-Members of the team who are responsible for graphic design
- Team members who are responsible for providing real and credible data related to the project idea that will be inserted into the application
- Team members who are responsible for developing flyers and advertising videos describing their project.

Each Institute must send a list containing the names of the students (with their CIN) who will participate in the competition before 10 March 2018 on the mail "sitic.bama.2018@gmail.com". The number of students should not exceed 6 people. A form will be sent to the universities to fill in the necessary details about the participating teams.

Annexe



The African market is considered a considerable asset with great potential for exploitation;
For example, sub-Saharan Africa will have the largest global growth in terms of import volumes over the next five years.

Thus, it will have a strong wave of urbanization (from 472 million people in 2015 to 1.4 billion in 2050) and a growth of the middle class (a surety of 125 million people between 2015 and 2024) that change the landscape population of the region.

Depending on this, Tunisia is likely to be able to benefit from these transformations:
3 of the 5 main products imported by Africa are also included in the top 10 of Tunisian exports. This competition allows students to view the African market as a promising market in the future.

It also allows them to show their creativity and share innovative ideas with a well-targeted audience (African experts). The student or the young entrepreneur or start-uper can through such competitions to confront the professional world and promote his ideas to investors on the continent.

This will be able to present Tunisia as incubator countries for young Talent in ICT "African Hub of ICT".
